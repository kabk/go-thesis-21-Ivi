Ritual of Death
Re-visualisation

1

SAY

Take a second to reflect,
and use your words to say:


"I am going to die"

2

REMEMBER

Take a moment, close your eyes


Think of all the moments you have had in life
(emphasize on the great ones)

3

WRITE

Write your own obituary


Explore the ways in which you want to be
remembered and celebrated

4

PLAN

Plan your death


From your outfit, to your funeral,
to the food that will be eaten
Plan and discover the endlessly beautiful possibilities

5

ENVISION

Envision all the things that death could therefore be
...

6

INVITE

Put both of your hands on your heart, and close your eyes

take in all the reflections, analysations,
thoughts and invite death in your life





















THE END!!!?
